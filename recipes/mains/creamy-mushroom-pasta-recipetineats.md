# Creamy Mushroom Pasta (RecipeTin Eats)

Source: https://www.recipetineats.com/creamy-mushroom-pasta/
~15 min · ~2 servings

## Ingredients
- Long pasta (fettuccine or linguine preferred) — ~80g uncooked per person
- Mushrooms — generous 300g/10oz for 2 servings (Swiss brown/cremini preferred; button OK)
- Garlic
- Butter AND oil (oil lets mushrooms brown without burning butter)
- White wine (to deglaze — adds depth, not winey)
- Cream
- Parmesan (thickens + flavour)
- Salt, pepper, parsley (optional)

## Method
1. Cook pasta in salted water; reserve some pasta water.
2. Pan-fry mushrooms in butter + oil until golden brown. Add garlic.
3. Deglaze with white wine, let it cook off.
4. Add cream, simmer, stir in parmesan to thicken.
5. Toss pasta through sauce, loosen with pasta water as needed. Season + serve.
