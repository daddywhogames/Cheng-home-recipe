# Korean Beef Bulgogi (Damn Delicious)

Source: https://damndelicious.net/2019/04/21/korean-beef-bulgogi/
Note: page was Cloudflare-blocked on fetch; this is a standard version of their bulgogi. Verify against source for exact amounts.

## Ingredients
Marinade:
- Soy sauce
- Brown sugar
- Sesame oil
- Garlic (minced)
- Ginger (grated)
- Gochujang (Korean chili paste) — optional for heat
- Sriracha — optional

Beef:
- Thinly sliced ribeye (or sirloin)
- Green onions
- Sesame seeds
- Oil for cooking

## Method
1. Whisk marinade: soy sauce, brown sugar, sesame oil, garlic, ginger (+ gochujang/sriracha if using).
2. Marinate thinly sliced beef ≥30 min (or overnight).
3. Heat oil in a hot skillet; cook beef in batches until browned and slightly caramelized.
4. Garnish with green onions + sesame seeds. Serve with steamed rice.
