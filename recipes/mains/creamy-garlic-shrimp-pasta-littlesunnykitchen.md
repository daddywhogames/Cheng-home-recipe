# Creamy Garlic Shrimp Pasta (Little Sunny Kitchen)

Source: https://littlesunnykitchen.com/garlic-shrimp-pasta/
~15 min

## Ingredients
- Shrimp (raw, peeled + deveined; any size)
- Long pasta (linguine)
- Olive oil + butter
- Garlic
- Heavy cream (or half-and-half — thinner)
- Parmesan (good quality)
- Optional seasoning: Old Bay / Cajun / smoked paprika
- Salt, pepper, parsley
- Reserved pasta cooking water

## Method
1. Boil + salt water; cook pasta to al dente. Reserve some pasta water.
2. Sauté shrimp in olive oil + butter + garlic until just cooked; set aside (don't overcook).
3. Add cream to the pan, simmer; stir in parmesan to thicken. Loosen with pasta water.
4. Return shrimp, toss pasta through the sauce. Season (Old Bay/Cajun optional), finish with parsley.
