# Chinese Braised Pork Trotters 红烧猪蹄 (Omnivore's Cookbook)

Source: https://omnivorescookbook.com/hardcore-chinese-braised-pork-feet/

Fall-off-the-bone tender, rich and savory, not greasy. Great over steamed rice.

## Ingredients
- Pork trotters, cut into bite-size pieces (butcher cuts ~6 per trotter)
- Aromatics: ginger, green onion
- Spices: star anise, clove, dried chili pepper
- Sauce: dark soy sauce + light soy sauce, Shaoxing wine, rock sugar (or regular), salt

## Method
1. Start trotters in cold water with ginger. Bring to boil, skim off the brown scum.
2. Boil/parboil to clean, then drain.
3. Pressure cook (or long simmer) until very tender.
4. Braise with aromatics + spices + soy sauces + Shaoxing wine + rock sugar until rich and glossy; reduce sauce to coat.
5. Serve over rice. (Freezes well — make a big batch.)
