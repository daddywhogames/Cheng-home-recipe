# Jjajangmyeon 짜장면 — Korean Black Bean Noodles (Maangchi)

Source: https://www.maangchi.com/recipe/jjajangmyeon
Note: page was Cloudflare-blocked on fetch; this is a standard version of Maangchi's recipe. Verify against source for exact amounts.

## Ingredients
- Jjajangmyeon noodles (or thick wheat noodles)
- Pork belly or pork shoulder, diced
- Chunjang (Korean black bean paste)
- Vegetables: onion, zucchini, potato, cabbage
- Oil
- Sugar
- Cornstarch/potato starch (slurry to thicken)
- Cucumber (julienned, garnish)
- (Optional: oyster sauce, sesame oil)

## Method
1. Fry chunjang in a good amount of oil (lowers bitterness); set aside.
2. Stir-fry diced pork until cooked. Add diced onion, potato, zucchini, cabbage; stir-fry until softened.
3. Add fried chunjang + water + a little sugar; simmer until veg are tender.
4. Thicken with starch slurry to a glossy sauce.
5. Cook + drain noodles; top with sauce and garnish with julienned cucumber.
