# Braised Pork Belly — Hong Shao Rou 红烧肉 (Red House Spice, Easy Version)

Source: https://redhousespice.com/red-cooked-pork-belly/

Tender, gelatinous red-braised pork belly — salty, sweet, aromatic, umami. This easy version skips frying/caramelising sugar.

## Ingredients (typical for this easy version)
- Pork belly (skin-on), cut into chunks
- Ginger
- Scallions
- Light soy sauce
- Dark soy sauce (for color)
- Shaoxing rice wine
- Rock sugar (or regular sugar)
- Spices: star anise, optional cinnamon stick, bay leaf
- Water

## Method
1. Blanch pork belly chunks in boiling water briefly; drain.
2. Add pork to pot with ginger, scallion, light + dark soy sauce, Shaoxing wine, rock sugar, spices, and water to nearly cover.
3. Bring to boil, then simmer covered ~45-60 min until tender.
4. Uncover and reduce the liquid over higher heat until glossy and thickened, coating the pork. Serve with rice.
