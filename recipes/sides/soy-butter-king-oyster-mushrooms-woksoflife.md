# Soy Butter Glazed King Oyster Mushrooms (The Woks of Life)

Source: https://thewoksoflife.com/soy-butter-glazed-king-oyster-mushrooms/

## Ingredients (typical for this dish)
- King oyster mushrooms (meaty, low liquid)
- Butter
- Soy sauce (light)
- Garlic
- (Often: a little sugar, black pepper, scallion to garnish)

## Method
1. Slice king oyster mushrooms (rounds or score the surface).
2. Pan-fry in butter until golden brown on both sides.
3. Add garlic, then soy sauce to glaze; toss until coated and glossy.
4. Garnish with scallion; serve.
