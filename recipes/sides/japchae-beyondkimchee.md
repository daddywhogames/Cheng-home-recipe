# Japchae — Korean Glass Noodle Stir-Fry (Beyond Kimchee)

Source: https://www.beyondkimchee.com/japchae/
~35 min

## Ingredients
- Dangmyeon (Korean sweet potato glass noodles)
- Beef (thinly sliced sirloin/striploin) — can be vegetarian
- Vegetables: onion, carrot, bell pepper, mushrooms, spinach
- Sauce: soy sauce, sugar, mirin, garlic, sesame oil
- Sesame seeds (garnish)

## Method
1. Soak dangmyeon in hot water ~15 min, drain (don't boil — keeps chewy).
2. Marinate beef in soy sauce + sugar + garlic + sesame oil.
3. Mix japchae sauce (soy/sugar/mirin/garlic/sesame oil) in advance.
4. Stir-fry firmer veg first, then mushrooms + spinach last to keep vibrant. Cook beef.
5. Toss noodles in pan with sauce + all ingredients until coated. Garnish with sesame seeds. Serve warm or room temp.
