# Everyday Vegetable Stir-Fry (The Woks of Life)

Source: https://thewoksoflife.com/everyday-vegetable-stir-fry/
Note: page was captcha-blocked on fetch; this is a standard version of their flexible stir-fry. Verify against source.

## Ingredients
- Mixed vegetables (whatever's on hand: broccoli, carrot, bell pepper, snap peas, bok choy, mushrooms, etc.)
- Garlic (and/or ginger)
- Oil (neutral, high smoke point)
- Stir-fry sauce: light soy sauce, oyster sauce, a little sugar, sesame oil, cornstarch + water slurry, splash of Shaoxing wine (see thewoksoflife.com/stir-fry-sauce-recipe)

## Method
1. Prep veg into even pieces; mix stir-fry sauce in a bowl.
2. Heat wok hot, add oil + garlic/ginger until fragrant.
3. Add firmer veg first, stir-fry; add quicker-cooking veg after.
4. Pour in sauce, toss until it thickens and coats. Serve hot.
