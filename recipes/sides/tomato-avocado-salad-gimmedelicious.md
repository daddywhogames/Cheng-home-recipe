# Easy Tomato Avocado Salad (Gimme Delicious)

Source: https://gimmedelicious.com/avocado-tomato-salad/

## Ingredients
- 2 cups chopped tomatoes (~1/2")
- 2 ripe avocados, cubed
- 1/4 small red onion, sliced
- 1/4 cup minced cilantro (or parsley)
- Juice of 1 lime
- 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
- 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
- Salt + black pepper
- Optional add-ins: cucumber, chickpeas, black beans, feta/mozzarella

## Method
1. Combine tomatoes, avocado, red onion, cilantro in a bowl.
2. Drizzle olive oil + lime juice; add red pepper flakes, salt, pepper. Gently toss.
3. Serve immediately (or chill up to 2 hrs).
