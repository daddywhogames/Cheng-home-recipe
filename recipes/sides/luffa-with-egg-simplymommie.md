# Luffa with Egg 絲瓜炒蛋 (Simply Mommie)

Source: https://simplymommie.com/2010/01/luffa-with-egg-recipe.html
Serves 2 · Prep 10 min · Cook 10 min

## Ingredients
- 1 luffa (skinned, spongy core removed, sliced thinly at an angle)
- 4 prawns (shelled, keep tail, deveined)
- 1 egg, beaten
- 1-2 cloves garlic, minced
- Stock or water
- Salt / light soy sauce to taste
- 2 tsp oil (1 for prawns, 1 for main)
- 1/2 tsp light soy sauce, dash sesame oil, dash white pepper

## Method
1. Sauté half the garlic in 1 tsp oil. Add prawns, cook until 80% done, remove.
2. Add remaining oil + garlic. Add luffa, fry 30 sec, add stock/water, simmer until luffa cooked.
3. Return prawns, simmer until cooked through.
4. Swirl in beaten egg slowly, bit by bit.
5. Season with salt/light soy. Serve.
