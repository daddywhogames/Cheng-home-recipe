# Mashed Sweet Potatoes (Martha Stewart)

Source: https://www.marthastewart.com/mashed-sweet-potatoes-recipe-11922486
Note: page was paywalled (402) on fetch; this is a standard version. Verify against source.

## Ingredients (typical)
- Sweet potatoes, peeled + cubed
- Butter
- Milk or cream
- Salt, pepper
- Optional: brown sugar, cinnamon/nutmeg, maple syrup

## Method
1. Boil sweet potato cubes in salted water until fork-tender (~15-20 min); drain.
2. Mash with butter and warm milk/cream to desired consistency.
3. Season with salt, pepper (and optional sweet spices). Serve warm.
