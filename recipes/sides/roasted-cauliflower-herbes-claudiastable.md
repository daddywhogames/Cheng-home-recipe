# Roasted (Purple) Cauliflower with Herbes de Provence (Claudia's Table)

Source: https://claudiastable.com/roasted-purple-cauliflower/
Serves 4 · Prep 5 min · Cook 20 min

## Ingredients
- 1 head cauliflower (purple or white), sliced
- 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
- 2 tsp dried Herbes de Provence
- Salt, pepper

## Method
1. Preheat oven 400°F. Line a rimmed baking sheet with parchment.
2. Spread cauliflower, drizzle olive oil, season with salt, pepper, Herbes de Provence.
3. Roast 20-25 min until tender and edges caramelized.
