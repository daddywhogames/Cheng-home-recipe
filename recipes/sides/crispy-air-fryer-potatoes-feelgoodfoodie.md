# Crispy Seasoned Air Fryer Potatoes (Feel Good Foodie)

Source: https://feelgoodfoodie.net/recipe/air-fryer-potatoes/

## Ingredients
- 1.5 lbs Yukon gold potatoes, quartered
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp salt (plus more to taste)
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- Cooking spray

## Method
1. Preheat air fryer to 380°F.
2. Toss potatoes with olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, salt, pepper.
3. Arrange in single layer in air fryer (may need 2 batches). Cook 20-25 min, shaking basket halfway, until crispy with golden edges.
4. Salt to taste and serve.
