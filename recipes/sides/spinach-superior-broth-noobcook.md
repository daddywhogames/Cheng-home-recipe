# Chinese Spinach in Superior Broth (Noob Cook)

Source: https://noobcook.com/spinach-in-superior-broth/2/
Serves 2 · Prep 15 min · Cook 20 min

## Ingredients
- 250g Chinese spinach (sharp 苋菜 or round 菠菜)
- 50g ikan bilis (dried anchovies), rinsed
- 500 ml water
- 1 tbsp cooking oil
- 2 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
- 1/2 century egg, diced (optional — makes soup murky)
- 1/2 tsp sugar
- 1 tbsp Shaoxing wine
- 1 tbsp wolfberries (goji), soaked 15 min, drained
- Light soy sauce to taste (optional)

## Method
1. Stock: simmer ikan bilis (in soup pouch) in 500ml water 10-15 min. Discard pouch, keep stock.
2. Separate spinach leaves from stems (de-string stems, cut 2cm).
3. Heat oil, fry garlic until lightly brown. Add spinach stems, stir-fry high 1 min, then leaves until just wilted.
4. Add ikan bilis stock, century egg, sugar, wolfberries; simmer ~1 min.
5. Add Shaoxing wine, let evaporate seconds, turn off. Season with light soy if needed.
