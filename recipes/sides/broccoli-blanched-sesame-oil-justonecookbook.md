# Broccoli Blanched with Sesame Oil ブロッコリーの塩ごま油茹で (Just One Cookbook)

Source: https://www.justonecookbook.com/broccoli-blanched-with-sesame-oil/

Blanching in salted water + sesame oil seasons the broccoli AND helps it keep flavor/texture longer in the fridge — great for meal prep.

## Ingredients
- Broccoli (florets + peeled stems — stems are edible & nutritious)
- Water
- Salt
- Toasted sesame oil

## Method
1. Bring water to a boil; add salt and a drizzle of sesame oil.
2. Blanch broccoli florets + sliced stems until crisp-tender.
3. Drain. The broccoli stays coated in sesame oil — serve right away or store for meal prep.
4. (Drizzle extra sesame oil if you like a stronger flavor.)

Works with: asparagus, bean sprouts, bok choy, cabbage, napa, spinach.
