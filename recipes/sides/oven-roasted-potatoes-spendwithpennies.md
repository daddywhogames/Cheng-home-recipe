# Simple Herb Oven Roasted Potatoes (Spend with Pennies)

Source: https://www.spendwithpennies.com/simple-herb-oven-roasted-potatoes/

## Ingredients
- Potatoes (red, Yukon gold, or russet)
- Olive oil
- Salt, pepper
- Herbs/seasoning (fresh or dried — e.g. rosemary, thyme, garlic powder, paprika)
- Optional toppings: parmesan, chives/green onion, sour cream

## Method
1. Wash + cut potatoes into 1" cubes.
2. (Optional) soak in cold water 20-60 min to remove starch; dry well.
3. Preheat oven 425°F. Toss potatoes with olive oil + seasonings/herbs.
4. Roast until tender and crisp/golden, tossing once (~30-40 min depending on size).
