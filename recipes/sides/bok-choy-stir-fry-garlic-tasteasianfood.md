# Bok Choy Stir-Fry with Garlic (Taste of Asian Food)

Source: https://tasteasianfood.com/bok-choy-stir-fry/

## Ingredients (classic Cantonese garlic bok choy)
- Bok choy (baby left whole; large cut into pieces)
- Garlic, minced/sliced
- Oil (neutral, high smoke point)
- Light soy sauce / oyster sauce
- A little sugar
- Salt
- Splash of water or stock
- (Optional: Shaoxing wine, sesame oil)

## Method
1. Wash bok choy; keep baby bok choy whole, separate stalks/leaves for large.
2. Heat wok very hot, add oil + garlic until fragrant.
3. Add bok choy stalks first (need more time), then leaves.
4. Add splash of water/stock + soy/oyster sauce + pinch sugar; stir-fry on high heat for wok aroma until just crisp-tender.
5. Serve immediately.
