# Easy Healthy Baked Salmon — Lemon & Garlic (Creme de la Crumb)

Source: https://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/best-easy-healthy-baked-salmon/
Note: also covers the duplicate "Easy Oven Baked Salmon" / "Oven Baked Salmon Lemon & Garlic" share links.

## Ingredients
- Salmon fillets
- Olive oil
- Salt, cracked black pepper
- Minced garlic
- Italian herb seasoning (or Herbs de Provence)
- Lemon (juice + slices)
- Fresh thyme or parsley (garnish, optional)

## Method
1. Preheat oven 400°F, grease baking pan. Arrange salmon, season with salt + pepper.
2. Stir olive oil + garlic + herbs + juice of 1/2 lemon; spoon over salmon, coating tops + sides.
3. Top each fillet with a thin lemon slice.
4. Bake 12-15 min until opaque and flaky. Optionally broil last 1-2 min.
5. Garnish and serve.
