# Hong Kong Style Steamed Cod (Honest Cooking)

Source: https://honestcooking.com/hong-kong-style-steamed-cod-fish/
~20-30 min

## Ingredients
- 1 lb (454g) fresh cod steak
- 2 slices ginger
- Dash white pepper
- 1 tbsp light soy sauce
- 1 tsp rock sugar (or caster sugar)
- 1 tbsp water
- 1 tbsp Shaoxing wine
- 3 cloves garlic
- 1 tbsp cooking oil
- 1 spring onion

## Method
1. Clean + pat cod dry. Lay ginger slices on heatproof dish, cod on top.
2. Steam over boiling water ~7 min (thicker fillets longer) until opaque + flaky. Pour off liquid.
3. Sauce: heat light soy + rock sugar + water + Shaoxing wine until sugar dissolves; keep warm.
4. Garlic oil: fry chopped garlic in oil until golden.
5. Sprinkle white pepper on fish, pour sauce over, then hot garlic oil (it sizzles).
6. Garnish with spring onion (soak in ice water to curl). Serve immediately.
