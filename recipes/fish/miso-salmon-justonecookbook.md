# Miso Salmon 味噌サーモン (Just One Cookbook)

Source: https://www.justonecookbook.com/miso-salmon/

## Ingredients
- Salmon (skin-on, ~1 inch / 2.5 cm thick, center-cut ~6 oz pieces)
- White miso (shiro miso)
- Soy sauce
- Sake (or Chinese rice wine / dry sherry)
- Mirin
- Roasted sesame oil (small amount)
- Garnish: toasted black + white sesame seeds, chopped green onion

## Method
1. Mix miso + soy sauce + sake + mirin + sesame oil into marinade.
2. Marinate salmon fillets 1-2 hrs in fridge.
3. Wipe off marinade; broil 10-13 min until tender and golden (skin on).
4. Garnish with sesame seeds + green onion; serve.
