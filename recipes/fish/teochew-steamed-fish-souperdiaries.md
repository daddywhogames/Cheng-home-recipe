# Teochew Steamed Fish (Souper Diaries)

Source: https://souperdiaries.com/teochew-steamed-fish/

A light Teochew/Chaozhou-style steamed fish — sour, savory, slightly spicy clear broth. Relies on fresh fish.

## Ingredients (typical Teochew style)
- Fresh whole fish or fillet (e.g. pomfret, sea bass, garoupa)
- Salted plums (preserved sour plum)
- Shiitake mushrooms
- Soft tofu
- Tomatoes
- Salted mustard green (kiam chai)
- Ginger
- Shallot oil (drizzle)
- Light soy sauce, a little sugar
- Spring onion / chili (optional)

## Method
1. Lay fish on heatproof dish with ginger. Top with salted plum, shiitake, tofu, tomato wedges, salted mustard green.
2. Season lightly (light soy, splash water/stock, touch of sugar).
3. Steam over boiling water until fish is just flaky (~8-12 min depending on size).
4. Finish with a drizzle of shallot oil and garnish. Serve with the broth.
