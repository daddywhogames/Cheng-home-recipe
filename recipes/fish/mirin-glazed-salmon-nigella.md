# Mirin Glazed Salmon (Nigella — Nigella Express)

Source: https://www.nigella.com/recipes/mirin-glazed-salmon
Serves 4 · very fast

## Ingredients
- 60 ml mirin (1/4 cup)
- 50 g soft light brown sugar (1/4 cup)
- 60 ml soy sauce (1/4 cup)
- 4 salmon fillets
- 2 tbsp rice wine vinegar
- 1-2 spring onions, halved and shredded into fine strips

## Method
1. Mix mirin, brown sugar and soy sauce in a shallow dish big enough for all 4 fillets. Marinate salmon 3 min on first side, 2 min on second. Heat a large non-stick pan (dry).
2. Cook salmon in the hot dry pan 2 min, turn, add the marinade, cook another 2 min.
3. Remove salmon to serving plate. Add rice vinegar to the marinade left in the pan; warm through.
4. Pour the dark sweet-salty glaze over salmon; top with spring onion strips.
5. Serve with rice or noodles (sushi ginger optional). Leftovers make a great cold salad next day.
