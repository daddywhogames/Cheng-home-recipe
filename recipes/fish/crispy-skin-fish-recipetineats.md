## What it is (from page description)
- A beautiful piece of fish cooked crispy-skin, served with a **bean ragu** that doubles as starch, vegetable, and sauce.

## General crispy-skin fish technique (estimate, for grocery use)
- White fish fillets with skin on
- Salt, pepper
- Oil
- Beans (e.g. cannellini/butter beans), garlic, tomato, herbs for the ragu

Pat skin dry, salt, sear skin-side down in hot oiled pan undisturbed until crisp, flip briefly. Serve over warm bean ragu.
