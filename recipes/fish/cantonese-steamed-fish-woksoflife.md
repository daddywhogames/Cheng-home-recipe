# Cantonese Steamed Fish (The Woks of Life) — 20 min

Source: https://thewoksoflife.com/cantonese-steamed-fish/

## Ingredients (classic Cantonese steamed fish)
- White fish fillet or whole fish (e.g. sea bass, cod, tilapia, snapper)
- Fresh ginger (julienned)
- Scallions (julienned)
- Cilantro (optional)
- Light soy sauce / seasoned steamed-fish soy sauce
- Sugar (small pinch)
- Hot water (to thin sauce)
- Neutral oil (heated, poured over at the end)
- Salt, white pepper

## Method
1. Pat fish dry, lay on heatproof dish with some ginger.
2. Steam over boiling water: thin fillets ~4-5 min; thicker/whole fish longer, until just flaky.
3. Pour off accumulated liquid. Top fish with fresh ginger + scallion (+ cilantro).
4. Warm soy sauce + sugar + a little hot water; pour around the fish.
5. Heat oil until smoking, pour over the aromatics so they sizzle. Serve immediately.
