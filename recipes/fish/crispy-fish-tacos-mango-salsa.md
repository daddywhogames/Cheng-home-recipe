# Crispy Fish Tacos with Fresh Mango Salsa (Real Food with Sarah)

Source: https://www.realfoodwithsarah.com/crispy-fish-tacos-with-mango-salsa/
4 servings · Prep 15 min · Cook 15 min

## Ingredients
Fish:
- White fish (haddock, cod, or mahi mahi)
- Flour + spice
- Eggs
- Breadcrumbs (panko)

Salsa & assembly:
- Mango (fresh ripe, or thawed frozen)
- (typical salsa: red onion, lime, cilantro, jalapeño — see source card)
- Tortillas
- Lettuce

## Method
1. Assembly line: flour+spice / beaten eggs / breadcrumbs in separate bowls.
2. Pat fish dry. Dredge in flour, then egg, then breadcrumbs (press to coat).
3. Place on rack over baking sheet; bake 400°F ~10 min until flaky. (Air fryer also works.)
4. Mix all salsa ingredients in a bowl.
5. Serve fish in warm tortillas with lettuce + mango salsa. Assemble just before eating.
