# Hamachi Kama — Grilled Yellowtail Collar カマ塩焼き (Just One Cookbook)

Source: https://www.justonecookbook.com/hamachi-kama/
~20 min

Yellowtail (hamachi) collar — the fatty, juicy part above the gills. Grilled/broiled until skin is crisp.

## Ingredients (typical shio-yaki style)
- Hamachi kama (yellowtail collar)
- Salt (sea salt)
- Sake (to rinse/season, optional)
- Citrus to serve: lemon / sudachi / daikon oroshi (grated radish)
- Soy sauce (for the grated radish, optional)

## Method
1. Pat collar dry; lightly salt both sides (optionally splash sake first, pat dry). Rest ~15 min, pat off moisture.
2. Broil/grill skin-side appropriately until skin is crisp and flesh just cooked through.
3. Serve with lemon wedge and grated daikon (+ soy sauce). Scrape flesh off cartilage with chopsticks.
