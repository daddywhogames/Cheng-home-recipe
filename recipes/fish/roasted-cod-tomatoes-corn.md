# Roasted Cod with Tomatoes and Corn (Cooking with Cocktail Rings)

Source: https://cookingwithcocktailrings.com/roasted-cod-with-tomatoes-and-corn/
Prep 10 min · Cook 20 min

## Ingredients
- Cod fillets (or tilapia/halibut)
- Shallot (or yellow/red onion)
- Cherry tomatoes
- Corn kernels (fresh, or canned/frozen)
- Lemon (juice)
- Fresh basil + chives
- Butter / olive oil
- Salt, pepper

## Method
1. Cook shallot, cherry tomatoes and corn in a pan until tomatoes are jammy — creates a built-in buttery sauce.
2. Nestle cod into the pan; roast in oven until cod is tender and flaky.
3. Finish with lemon juice, basil, chives.
4. Serve over rice / with crusty bread to soak up sauce.
