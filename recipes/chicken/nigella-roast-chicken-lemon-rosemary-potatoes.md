# Nigella Lawson's Roast Chicken with Lemon, Rosemary, Garlic & Potatoes

Source: https://www.smh.com.au/goodfood/roast-chicken-with-lemon-rosemary-garlic-and-potatoes-20151211-gll6s4.html
(from "Simply Nigella")

## Ingredients
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 2 tsp finely chopped rosemary (plus more to serve)
- 1 bulb garlic, separated into unpeeled cloves
- 2 leeks
- 1 kg waxy potatoes (e.g. kipfler), unpeeled
- 2 unwaxed lemons
- 1 medium chicken (~1.4 kg), preferably organic
- Sea salt flakes

## Method
1. Preheat oven 220°C. Pour all but ~1 tsp oil into a large roasting tin. Add rosemary + garlic cloves.
2. Trim leeks, halve lengthways, slice into half-moons; add to tin.
3. Cut potatoes into 1.5cm slices, then quarter; add to tin.
4. Quarter lemons then halve each quarter, remove pips, toss in. Mix everything; clear a space for the chicken.
5. Untruss chicken, sit it in the space, pour remaining oil over it, salt the chicken only.
6. Roast 1 hr 10 min until juices run clear at thigh joint. Rest chicken on board (let cavity juices spill into tin); return potatoes to oven ~10 min until soft + golden.
