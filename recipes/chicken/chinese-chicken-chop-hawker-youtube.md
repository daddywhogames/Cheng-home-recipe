## Ingredients
Chicken + marinade:
- Boneless chicken leg/thigh (skin-on)
- Light soy sauce, oyster sauce
- Sugar, white pepper
- Sesame oil
- Garlic
- Cornflour (for coating, optional pan-fry crispness)

Black pepper / brown sauce (hawker-style):
- Butter
- Onion, garlic
- Worcestershire sauce
- Oyster sauce / dark soy sauce
- Ketchup or tomato sauce
- Sugar
- Black pepper (coarse)
- Water/stock + cornflour slurry

Sides (typical hawker plate):
- Fries, baked beans, coleslaw, grilled tomato

## Method
1. Marinate chicken leg in soy/oyster sauce, sugar, pepper, sesame oil, garlic ≥30 min.
2. Pan-fry (or grill) skin-side down until skin is crisp and chicken cooked through. Rest, then slice.
3. Sauce: sauté onion + garlic in butter; add Worcestershire, oyster/dark soy, ketchup, sugar, black pepper, water/stock; thicken with cornflour slurry.
4. Plate chicken with fries/beans/coleslaw; pour black pepper sauce over. Serve.
