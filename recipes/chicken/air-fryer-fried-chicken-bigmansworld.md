# Air Fryer Fried Chicken — KFC Style (The Big Man's World)

Source: https://thebigmansworld.com/air-fryer-fried-chicken/

## Ingredients
- Bone-in, skin-on chicken (mix of thighs, drumsticks, breasts)
- Buttermilk
- Spices for marinade: salt, garlic powder, smoked paprika, pepper

Breading:
- Flour (all-purpose or wheat)
- Cornstarch (key for crisp)
- Salt, black pepper, paprika, onion powder, garlic powder
- Olive oil spray

## Method
1. Marinate chicken in buttermilk + salt/pepper/smoked paprika/garlic powder ≥10 min.
2. Mix flour + cornstarch + spices for breading.
3. Remove chicken from buttermilk, shake off excess, dredge in flour mix (coat both sides).
4. Spray air fryer basket with oil; air fry until crispy and cooked through.
