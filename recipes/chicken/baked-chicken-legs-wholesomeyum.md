# Baked Chicken Legs (Wholesome Yum)

Source: https://www.wholesomeyum.com/crispy-baked-chicken-legs-drumsticks-recipe/

## Ingredients
- Chicken drumsticks (uniform size)
- Butter or oil (ghee, olive, or avocado oil; cooking spray works)
- Smoked paprika
- Garlic powder
- Salt, pepper
- (Optional swaps: Italian seasoning, lemon pepper, Cajun, oregano, onion powder, cayenne)

## Method
1. Preheat oven; line baking sheet with foil, set oven-safe rack on top.
2. Pat drumsticks dry, brush with melted butter/oil, sprinkle spices all over.
3. Roast at high heat, flipping halfway, until crisp and cooked through (165°F/74°C internal).
4. Rest a few minutes before serving.
