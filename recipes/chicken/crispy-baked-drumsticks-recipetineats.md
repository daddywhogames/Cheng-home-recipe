# Crispy Baked Chicken Drumsticks (RecipeTin Eats)

Source: https://www.recipetineats.com/crispy-baked-chicken-drumsticks/

## Ingredients
- Chicken drumsticks (wings or skin-on bone-in thighs also work)
- Baking powder (NOT baking soda) — for crispy skin
- Smoked or plain paprika
- Onion powder
- Garlic powder
- Plain flour (bulks out seasoning to coat evenly)
- Cooking oil + oil spray
- Salt, pepper

## Method
1. Mix flour, baking powder, paprika, onion + garlic powder, salt in a shallow bowl.
2. Coat drumsticks in oil (hands), stretch skin over flesh.
3. Dredge in seasoning, shake off excess.
4. Place on rack over foil-lined tray, spray generously with oil.
5. Bake 30 min at 200°C/390°F (180°C fan), rotate once. Then crank to 220°C/430°F and bake 15 min more until crisp & golden.
