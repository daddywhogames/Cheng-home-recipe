# Roast Chicken (RecipeTin Eats)

Source: https://www.recipetineats.com/roast-chicken/

## Ingredients
- Whole chicken (~2 kg)
- Butter (softened)
- Garlic (minced, for butter)
- Fresh herbs (e.g. rosemary, thyme, parsley)
- Lemon (wedges for cavity + zest/juice in butter)
- Onion + extra garlic (bed for the pan)
- Salt, pepper

## Method
1. Make garlic-herb-lemon butter. Loosen skin with an upside-down spoon; spread most butter under skin, rest on top.
2. Stuff cavity with lemon wedges + herb sprigs.
3. Sit chicken on a bed of onion + garlic in roasting pan.
4. Roast: 10 min at 220°C/450°F, then 1 hr 15 min at 180°C/350°F (formula: 20 min per 500g) until internal 75°C/165°F / juices run clear.
5. Serve with pan juices (liquid gold) or make gravy.
