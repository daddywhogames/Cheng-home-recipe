# Soy Sauce Chicken Wings 紅燒雞翼 (The Hong Kong Cookery)

Source: https://www.thehongkongcookery.com/2012/04/soy-sauce-chicken-wings.html
Makes ~14 wings · Prep 2 min · Cook 30 min

## Ingredients
- 2 lbs chicken wings
- 4 slices ginger (1/8" thick)
- 3 spring onions, white part only, cut into 1" pieces
- 1 tbsp peanut oil
- 2 tbsp light soy sauce
- 1.5 tbsp dark soy sauce
- 3 lumps rock sugar (~1/2" each)
- 3 star anise

## Method
1. Heat wok med-high, add oil, ginger and spring onion; stir-fry until fragrant.
2. Add wings, stir to coat, stir-fry 3-4 min until light gold on all sides.
3. Add water to barely cover + both soy sauces, rock sugar, star anise. Cover, simmer 20 min on med-low, stirring occasionally.
4. Remove wings; reduce stock over high heat until glossy and thick. Pour over wings and serve. Good hot or cold.
