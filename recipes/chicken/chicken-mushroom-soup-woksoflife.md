# Chinese Chicken Mushroom Soup (The Woks of Life)

Source: https://thewoksoflife.com/chinese-chicken-mushroom-soup/

## Ingredients
- Whole young chicken (or small organic chicken)
- Dried shiitake mushrooms (more flavorful than fresh)
- Goji berries (don't overdo — turns soup sweet)
- Dried Chinese red dates (jujube)
- Ginger
- Water (~8 cups)
- Salt (add right before serving so chicken stays moist)

## Method
1. Wash + rinse dried shiitakes, soak in 8 cups water 6 hrs/overnight. Trim stems, return mushrooms to pot.
2. Add goji berries + Chinese dates to the soak water/pot.
3. Add chicken + ginger; simmer until chicken is cooked and broth is flavorful.
4. (Optional: trim chicken breast for another dish so it doesn't dry out.)
5. Salt right before serving.
