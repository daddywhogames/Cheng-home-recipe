# Honey Soy Chicken Wings (Rasa Malaysia)

Source: https://rasamalaysia.com/honey-soy-chicken-wings/

## Ingredients
- Chicken wings (drumettes/mid-sections)
- White sesame seeds (garnish)

Marinade:
- Soy sauce
- Cooking sake (or rice wine)
- Sesame oil
- Scallion
- Black pepper
- Ginger

Honey soy glaze:
- Honey
- Soy sauce
- Mirin (or rice vinegar + sugar)
- Cooking sake

## Method
1. Marinate wings in soy/sake/sesame oil/scallion/pepper/ginger.
2. Bake until crispy outside, juicy inside.
3. Coat in honey soy glaze; bake/broil until caramelized and sticky.
4. (Drumsticks variation: ~30-40 min at 400°F, flip halfway, internal 165°F.)
